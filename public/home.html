Welcome to Language Matches

Looking to practice language through conversation? Want to help other students learn the language you love? If you are an undergraduate or graduate student, find a language partner at Brown by signing up for Language Matches! Enter the language you speak and/or want to learn, and we'll try to find a match for you!

OR

Please note: This site is open only to Brown University undergraduate and graduate students. We do not screen our members, and the information found in the profiles is created by the members, and is not screened or moderated by Brown or the site administrator. It is the responsibility of the members to reach out to their language partner(s) to set up meeting times. Terms of Use.
